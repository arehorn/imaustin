The Problem

Standard weather apps show you a single prediction: "70° and sunny." But that's not how weather works. You need to know: what's the chance of rain during my lake day?

The Solution

HolliWeather visualizes probability distributions, not point predictions. See the confidence levels. Plan better vacations. Actually trust your forecast.
